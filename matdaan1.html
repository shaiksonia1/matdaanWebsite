ONLINE VOTING

A way towards digitalized India

The main motive of creating MatDaan (e-voting) is to fulfill over the government word of maintaining the social distancing in this pandemic of covid-19.
As MatDaan will give people to cast their votes by sitting at their place and also not doing the gathering over a place. Not only in this pandemic, application is useful after this covid-19. People who are disabled or not able to go and cast their vote MatDaan will give them a platform to vote.

An electronic voting system which is used nowadays provide some characteristic different from the traditional voting technique, and also it provides improved features of voting system over traditional voting system such as accuracy, convenience, flexibility, privacy, verifiability and mobility. E-voting solutions can increase the security of the ballot, speed up the processing of results and make voting easier. This is a voting system by which any voter can use his/her voting rights from anywhere in the country
